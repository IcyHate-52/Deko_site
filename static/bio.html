Denis "Deko" Zhukov

Biography Denis Zhukov was born on July 10, 2001, in Naberezhnye Chelny.

Esports Career Denis "deko" Zhukov began his esports career in 2017. In 2020, deko played for various teams, including QBF, Quantum Bellator Fire, and EXTREMUM. On January 18, 2021, he signed with 1win Team. Later, deko took 3rd and 4th place at the Dell Gaming League Season 2 and Spring Sweet Spring #3 tournaments. In September of that year, the team won gold at the Malta Vibes Knockout Series #2 ($24,000). In October 2024, 1win Team took 2nd place at the Malta Vibes Knockout Series #3. In April and June 2022, deko and 1win Team took 2nd place at the ESL Challenger League Season 40: Europe and ESL Challenger League Season 41: Europe tournaments. In 2023, the team won bronze at the ESL Challenger League Season 44: Europe online tournament ($7,000). In April of the same year, 1win Team participated in the BLAST.tv Paris Major 2023: European RMR A, but did not place.

Aurora Gaming and the WW team On November 8, 2023, Denis "deko" Zhukov officially transferred to Aurora Gaming, which the club announced on social media. With his new organization, deko won the Skyesports Grand Slam 2024 and Skyesports Masters 2024, and also took second place in the ESL Challenger at DreamHack Melbourne 2024. Inactive In November 2024, Aurora Gaming announced changes to its Counter-Strike 2 roster: deko left the team's active roster and took a break from professional play. In January 2025, he participated in the Evelone Duo Cup media tournament, where he teamed with Andrey "tN1R" Tatarinovich. On November 16, 2025, Denis "deko" Zhukov, who was still on the bench of Aurora Gaming, joined the WW team.
