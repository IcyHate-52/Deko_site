Team History

  • Russian sniper Denis "deko" Zhukov has been competing in the professional Counter-Strike scene since 2017.
  • His main teams include:
  • Quantum Bellator Fire (QBF) / EXTREMUM (2020) — his first notable teams in the tier 2/3 scene.
  • •1win Team (January 2021 – November 2023) — the main period of his career, where he became known as a star sniper.
  • •Aurora Gaming (November 2023 – November 2024) — competing in major international tournaments.
  • •WW Team (November 2025 – present) — his current team.
  • The player also periodically participated in mixes and streamer tournaments (e.g., Ramzes Club, Sasavot Team, EGOISTO). In November 2024, he announced a temporary break from his professional career for the sake of streaming, but later returned to the game.
